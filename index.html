Join the decentralized healthcare revolution with Immunify.

Immunify is a game-changer in the healthcare industry, offering a decentralized platform that puts patients back in control of their health data. With the power of blockchain technology, Immunify allows for secure and transparent sharing of medical records and information, enabling patients to make informed decisions about their health and treatment options.

Open EHR Compliant

Immunify places a strong emphasis on providing an open EHR (Electronic Health Record) compliant platform. With a focus on interoperability, the platform ensures that healthcare data is easily accessible and transferable between different healthcare providers. This makes it easier for patients to keep track of their medical history and receive the best possible care.

Decentralised Data Storage on Cartesi

Immunify's decentralized data storage on Cartesi provides a secure and efficient way to store patient data. This eliminates the need for a central server and reduces the risk of data breaches. With Cartesi's technology, data is stored on a decentralized blockchain network, making it tamper-proof and resilient to attacks.

Security Ensured through Data Encryption

To ensure the security and privacy of patient data, Immunify uses advanced encryption techniques. All data stored on the platform is encrypted, making it difficult for unauthorized parties to access it. This ensures that patients have complete control over their data, and healthcare providers can securely access it when needed.

Decentralized Data Marketplace

Immunify's decentralized data marketplace creates a unique opportunity for patients to monetize their healthcare data. Patients can choose to sell their data to research institutions, pharmaceutical companies, and other healthcare providers, creating a new stream of income for individuals. This marketplace also allows for increased transparency in data usage and creates a more equitable distribution of wealth in the healthcare industry.
